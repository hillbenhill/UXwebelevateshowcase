Paddy Hi, I’m Paddy McGlinchey formerly of Team 29. I live in Donegal and I am a PM on the Haven project. When the brief for the Minor Project was released, we as a team carefully examined each of the three themes Using Coggle to brainstorm our thoughts and ideas, it was soon obvious that collectively we wanted to develop a product that could really make a difference to somebody’s life!
